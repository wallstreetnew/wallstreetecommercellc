Driven to Deliver
Life-Sustaining Dialysis Solutions

Rockwell Medical’s products are critical to vulnerable patients with end-stage kidney disease. Our mission is to provide dialysis clinics and the patients they serve with the highest quality products supported by the best customer service in the industry. 

Our dry and liquid citric acid, acetic acid, and bicarbonate hemodialysis products are manufactured under cGMP regulations at our facilities located throughout the United States.

CitraPure®, Dri-Sate®, RenalPure®, and SteriLyte® are registered trademarks of Rockwell.

Citric Acid Concentrate Powders and Liquids

CitraPure concentrate is citric acid-based and 100% acetate-free. CitraPure is packaged as a liquid and as a dry powder acid concentrate for use with Rockwell Medical’s Dry Acid Concentrate Mixer.

Liquid CitraPure is available in 55-gallon drums and 1-gallon (3.78L) jugs. Each case of dry product produces 25 gallons of CitraPure liquid acid concentrate. Product Details & Ordering Information

Acetic Acid Concentrate Powders and Liquids

RenalPure Liquid Concentrate is an acetic acid-based liquid product, available in 55-gallon drums or 1-gallon (3.78L) jugs.

Dri-Sate concentrate is an acetic acid-based powder product. DriSate is packaged as a dry powder acid concentrate for use with our Dry Acid Concentrate Mixer. Each Dri-Sate case produces 25 gallons of RenalPure liquid. Product Details & Ordering Information

Bicarbonate Concentrate Powders and Liquids

RenalPure bicarbonate is a dry powder mixed on-site at the clinic and is packaged for bulk use or individual treatment.

SteriLyte bicarbonate is a liquid available in 1-gallon (3.78L)  jugs. Product Details & Ordering Information

Dry Acid Concentrate Mixer and Accessories

Rockwell Medical’s Dry Acid Concentrate Mixer is designed for the Company’s CitraPure and Dri-Sate Dry Acid products and enables a dialysis clinic to mix acid concentrate on-site.

  • Rapid Mixing - Mixes 100 gallons of product in approximately 3 minutes
  • Quick and Easy Installation - Requires no custom plumbing
  • Compact size - Fits through standard door
  • Uses forced RO water pump to mix the product
  • Side opening makes it easy to clean and to pour dry mix
Refer to Manufacturer's Instructions for Use for detailed technical specifications. Product Details & Ordering Information

Concentrates Cleaner and Salt Pellets

Rockwell Medical offers essential concentrates accessories including cleaning agents, 5% Acetic Acid cleaning solution, 5% and 2% Dry Citric Acid Descale, Filtration Salts, and other supplies used by hemodialysis providers. Product Details & Ordering Information

*NEW* Convenience Pack

Rockwell Medical is pleased to announce our new Convenience Pack, which includes two 1-gallon pre-mixed containers of one of the following Rockwell Medical hemodialysis concentrates products, RenalPure or SteriLyte.

Our Convenience Pack offers patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers a number of advantages including more ergonomic packaging with cutout handles, half the weight of our standard packaging, easier shipping and handling, and streamlined workflow.
